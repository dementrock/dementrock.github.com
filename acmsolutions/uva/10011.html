ACM Solutions - UVa 10011

Jan 5th, 2012

Spent 2 days on this one. I tried different methods to test, and finally the following process worked:

  • Test if the line through origin and house passes through the circle and has two intersection points.

  • Test if the segment connecting house and center of tree intersects with at least one of the two tangent lines

  • Test if the house is within the triangle formed by the origin and two tangent points.

If all three tests pass, then pick the minimum among the distance from the house to the tree and the distance from the house from two tangent lines.
